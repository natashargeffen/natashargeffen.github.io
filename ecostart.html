EcoStart

The Problem: The everyday products we consume can impact the environmental in deterimental ways.


The Solution: EcoStart, A new eCommerce app that helps users easily find eco-friendly products.

Goals

  • Give people a platform that helps them easily find everyday products that don’t negatively impact the environment.
  • Give people resources to these products at prices they can afford.
  • Provide an “eco-rating” for each product, a rating system that calculates how eco-friendly the product is. This factors in how material is sourced, how it impacts the environment, and how the company makes environmentally-based decisions.
