TeaLink

TeaLink uses Information and Communications Technology (ICT) to enhance sustainability in Sri Lanka's tea farming community.

Goals

  • Create a platform that manages the tea leaf collection system and skilled labor pool.
  • Facilitate farmer-centered technology sharing between farmers and their neighbors.
  • Create job opportunities for youth by providing best practice resources and matching demand and supply of labor in the region.
